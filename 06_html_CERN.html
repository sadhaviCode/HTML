CERN

1. Introduction
CERN is dedicated to studying the fundamentalparticles and forces that make up the universe.
Scientists from many countries work together at CERN to conduct experiments and make new discoveries in physics.

2. Full Form
CERN stands for European Organization for Nuclear Research.

3. Invention of the World Wide Web
In 1989, Tim Berners-Lee invented the World Wide Web at CERN
to allow scientists to share information more easily.
